01 Introduction

Tekami Navigator ("the App") is Ethiopia's address navigation system developed by Tekami. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the App.

By using Tekami Navigator, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

02 Information We Collect

Account Information

When you register, we collect:

  • First name and father's name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Password (stored as a secure hash — never in plain text)

Location Data

The App uses your device's GPS to:

  • Determine your current position for navigation
  • Record GPS coordinates when you save an address to the database
  • Track your position while navigation is active
Location data is only used while the App is open and active. We do not collect location data in the background.

Address Data You Save

When you save an address (Data Collector role and above), we store:

  • Region, city, woreda, kebele, street, and house number
  • GPS coordinates of the saved location
  • Accuracy information of the GPS fix
  • The email of the user who collected the data (for accountability)

Arrival Feedback

When you complete navigation and confirm whether you arrived at the correct location, we record a correct or incorrect count against the saved coordinate. This data is anonymous and is used only to improve address accuracy.

03 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide turn-by-turn navigation using Ethiopian address data
  • To authenticate your account and control access by role
  • To allow authorized users to save and manage address data
  • To improve the accuracy of saved locations over time using arrival feedback
  • To maintain the integrity and security of the address database

04 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers hosted in the United States. We implement the following security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using BCrypt and never stored in plain text
  • All API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access to address-saving features is restricted by user role
  • JWT tokens are used for authentication with 24-hour expiry

05 Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your personal data with advertisers or marketing companies.

Address coordinate data may be used internally to improve the navigation system.

06 User Roles & Data Access

The App uses a role-based access system. Each role has different data access permissions:

Role Permissions
User Can navigate and search addresses. Cannot save addresses.
Data Collector Can save addresses and GPS coordinates to the database.
Admin Can manage Data Collector accounts and save addresses.
Super Admin Full system access including role management.

Only administrators can view which email address collected a specific location entry.

07 Account Deletion

You can delete your account directly from within the App — no email or waiting required:

  1. Open Tekami Navigator on your device
  2. Tap the ☰ menu button in the top-right corner
  3. Tap My Profile
  4. Tap Delete Account and confirm
When you delete your account, your personal information — name, email, phone number, and password — is permanently removed from our systems immediately. Address coordinates that you collected remain in the database but are no longer linked to your identity. This preserves the integrity of Ethiopia's address data for other users.

08 Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Delete your account and personal data at any time (see Section 7)
  • Withdraw consent to location access by disabling GPS permissions in your device settings

09 Children's Privacy

Tekami Navigator is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this document. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
